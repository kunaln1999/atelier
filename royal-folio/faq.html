How does one commission a bespoke piece?

The commissioning process is a collaborative journey of artistry. It begins with a private consultation, where your vision is discussed with our master artisans. Following this, sketches and gem selections are presented for your approval before our atelier brings your unique masterpiece to life.

Provenance of the gemstones

We adhere to the strictest standards of ethical sourcing. Each gemstone in our collection is accompanied by certification from globally recognized gemological institutes, ensuring its origin, authenticity, and conflict-free status. We curate only the rarest stones with exceptional clarity and fire.

Do you offer private consultations?

Indeed. We provide private, by-appointment viewings at our flagship boutiques or strictly confidential consultations at a location of your preference. Our concierge team is available to arrange these appointments to suit your schedule with the utmost discretion.

International shipping protocols

We offer secure, fully insured global delivery services. For items of significant value, we can arrange for personal courier delivery to ensure your acquisition reaches you with the utmost security. All protocols are handled with white-glove precision.

Caring for heirloom pieces

Heirloom pieces require delicate preservation. We recommend an annual professional inspection and cleaning by our atelier. For daily care, avoid exposure to harsh chemicals and store each piece individually in its provided velvet casing to prevent scratches.
