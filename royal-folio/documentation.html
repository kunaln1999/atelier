Standards

"A commitment to transparency, craftsmanship, and excellence. Review our policies and provenance with absolute clarity."

01

Returns & Refund Policy

We invite you to experience our creations with confidence. If an acquisition does not meet your expectations, we offer a transparent return process.

  • I. Eligibility Returns are accepted within 14 days of receipt. The piece must remain unworn, in its original pristine condition.
  • II. Packaging Must be returned with all original packaging, certificates of authenticity, and diamond grading reports.
  • III. Exceptions Bespoke commissions and personalized engravings are final sale and cannot be returned.
02

Material Sourcing & Ethics

Ethical Procurement

We strictly adhere to the Kimberley Process, ensuring every diamond is conflict-free. Our gold is sourced from refineries committed to responsible mining practices.

Artisan Transparency

Each piece is traced from mine to market. We maintain direct relationships with our lapidaries and master goldsmiths to ensure fair wages and safe working conditions.

03

Quality & Craftsmanship

Every creation undergoes a rigorous multi-stage inspection before leaving our atelier.

Step I

Stone Authentication

Gemological verification of clarity, color, and cut precision.

Step II

Structural Integrity

Microscopic setting checks and prong durability testing.

Step III

Hallmark & Certification

Laser inscription of purity hallmarks and issue of authenticity papers.

Step IV

Final Polish

High-gloss rhodium or gold finishing for flawless radiance.
